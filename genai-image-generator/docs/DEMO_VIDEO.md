## Production Notes

- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS
- **Audio**: Clear voiceover with background music

3. **Browser**: Chrome with developer tools ready

- **Safety**: Demonstrate content moderation
